# Trigram Reference

The eight trigrams (八卦, *bāguà*) are the fundamental building blocks of I Ching divination. Each trigram is a 3-line binary pattern corresponding to a natural element, a direction, a season, a family member, a body part, and an animal — a complete cosmological vocabulary encoded in 3 bits.

---

## The Eight Trigrams

### ☰ Heaven — Qián (乾)

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Binary | 111 (value 7) |
| Element | Metal / Sky |
| Quality | Creative, Strong, Active, Persevering |
| Direction (Later Heaven) | Northwest |
| Direction (Earlier Heaven) | South |
| Season | Late Autumn |
| Time of Day | Evening |
| Family | Father |
| Body Part | Head |
| Animal | Horse |
| Color | Gold [1.0, 0.92, 0.45] |
| Force Vector | Radial outward, upward [0, 1] |
| Lo Shu Number | 6 |
| Hexagram | Three solid lines |

**Nature**: Pure creative force. The dragon is its symbol. Heaven is not space but dynamic energy — the incessant movement that drives all things. Leibniz's 1 = 111 = fullness.

## The Two Arrangements

### Earlier Heaven (先天八卦, Xiān Tiān Bāguà) — Fu Xi Arrangement

The Earlier Heaven arrangement shows cosmological polarity: Heaven opposes Earth, Fire opposes Water, Thunder opposes Wind, Mountain opposes Lake. Each pair is a complement (XOR 7 = total opposite).

```
        Heaven (7)
   Wind (6)    Lake (3)
Fire (5)          Mountain (4)
   Thunder (1)  Water (2)
        Earth (0)
```

Reading clockwise from South: Heaven (S), Lake (SE), Fire (E), Thunder (NE), Earth (N), Mountain (NW), Water (W), Wind (SW).

**Mnemonic structure**: Opposite trigrams always sum to 7 (XOR = 7, which means all bits flipped). Heaven (111) ↔ Earth (000); Fire (101) ↔ Water (010); Wind (110) ↔ Thunder (001); Lake (011) ↔ Mountain (100).

### Later Heaven (後天八卦, Hòu Tiān Bāguà) — King Wen Arrangement

The Later Heaven arrangement encodes **seasonal and diurnal cycles** in space:

```
           Fire/Li (S) ☲
    Wind/Xun       Earth/Kun
   ☴ (SE)            (SW) ☷
Thunder/Zhen         Lake/Dui
☳ (E)                (W) ☱
   Mountain/Gen    Heaven/Qian
   ☶ (NE)           (NW) ☰
           Water/Kan (N) ☵
```

| Direction | Trigram | Binary | Season | Time |
|-----------|---------|--------|--------|------|
| South | Fire (Li) | 101 = 5 | Summer | Noon |
| Southwest | Earth (Kun) | 000 = 0 | Late Summer | — |
| West | Lake (Dui) | 011 = 3 | Autumn | Dusk |
| Northwest | Heaven (Qian) | 111 = 7 | Late Autumn | Evening |
| North | Water (Kan) | 010 = 2 | Winter | Midnight |
| Northeast | Mountain (Gen) | 100 = 4 | Late Winter | Pre-dawn |
| East | Thunder (Zhen) | 001 = 1 | Spring | Dawn |
| Southeast | Wind (Xun) | 110 = 6 | Late Spring | Morning |

The Later Heaven cycle describes the annual passage of seasons and the daily cycle of the sun. Reading clockwise from South, it traces: peak of summer (Fire) → harvest (Earth) → autumn completion (Lake) → winter preparation (Heaven) → depth of winter (Water) → first stirring (Mountain) → spring awakening (Thunder) → growth (Wind).

## Family Structure

The 8 trigrams encode a family system derived from Heaven (Father) and Earth (Mother):

| Generation | Member | Trigram | How formed |
|------------|--------|---------|------------|
| Parents | Father | Heaven (7) | Pure yang |
| Parents | Mother | Earth (0) | Pure yin |
| First | Eldest Son | Thunder (1) | Heaven's first yang breaks into Earth |
| First | Eldest Daughter | Wind (6) | Earth's first yin enters Heaven |
| Second | Middle Son | Water (2) | Heaven's second yang within Earth |
| Second | Middle Daughter | Fire (5) | Earth's second yin within Heaven |
| Third | Youngest Son | Mountain (4) | Heaven's third yang tops Earth |
| Third | Youngest Daughter | Lake (3) | Earth's third yin tops Heaven |

**Pattern**: Each son trigram has one yang received from Heaven; each daughter trigram has one yin received from Earth. The position of the received line (bottom, middle, top) determines birth order.

## Trigram Combinations: The 64 Hexagrams

Every pair of trigrams stacked (upper over lower) produces a hexagram. The formula:

```
hexagram_binary = (upper_trigram << 3) | lower_trigram
```

All 8 × 8 = 64 combinations give the complete system. Examples:

| Upper | Lower | Binary | Hexagram |
|-------|-------|--------|---------|
| Heaven (7) | Heaven (7) | 111111 = 63 | Qian (1) — The Creative |
| Earth (0) | Earth (0) | 000000 = 0 | Kun (2) — The Receptive |
| Water (2) | Fire (5) | 010101 = 21 | Ji Ji (63) — After Completion |
| Fire (5) | Water (2) | 101010 = 42 | Wei Ji (64) — Before Completion |
| Wind (6) | Thunder (1) | 110001 = 49 | Yi (42) — Increase |
| Thunder (1) | Wind (6) | 001110 = 14 | Heng (32) — Duration |

## Elemental Interactions in Shaders

The `_trigram_elemental.frag` shader implements these elemental forces:

```glsl
// Heaven: radial outward (creative expansion)
vec2 force = normalize(p) * 0.8 + vec2(0.0, 0.3);

// Earth: gravitational downward (receptive collection)
vec2 force = vec2(sin(p.x * 2.0) * 0.2, -0.9);

// Thunder: radial pulse (arousing shock)
float pulse = sin(r * 8.0 - t * 4.0);
vec2 force = normalize(p) * pulse;

// Water: downward flow with lateral diffusion
vec2 force = vec2(sin(p.y * 3.0 + t) * 0.4, -0.7);

// Mountain: zero field (keeping still)
vec2 force = vec2(0.0, 0.0);

// Wind: diagonal permeation
vec2 force = vec2(0.6 + 0.2 * sin(p.y * 4.0 + t), 0.3 * cos(p.x * 3.0));

// Fire: upward with shimmer
float shimmer = sin(p.x * 6.0 + t * 2.0) * 0.3;
vec2 force = vec2(shimmer, 0.9 + shimmer * 0.2);

// Lake: horizontal reflection
vec2 force = vec2(0.7, sin(p.x * 4.0 + t) * 0.3);
```

## The Lo Shu Square and Trigram Resonance

The **Lo Shu** (洛書) magic square encodes trigram relationships through number:

```
4  9  2
3  5  7
8  1  6
```

Every row, column, and diagonal sums to **15** (= 3 × 5, center × 3). The number 15 is the sum of the Lo Shu = 3 times its center = the "resonance constant" of the trigram system.

Mapping (Later Heaven):
- 1 → Water (Kan): north, deep
- 2 → Earth (Kun): southwest, receptive  
- 3 → Thunder (Zhen): east, arousing
- 4 → Wind (Xun): southeast, penetrating
- 5 → Center / Balance: no trigram, pure qi
- 6 → Heaven (Qian): northwest, creative
- 7 → Lake (Dui): west, joyous
- 8 → Mountain (Gen): northeast, still
- 9 → Fire (Li): south, clinging

Diagonals: Fire (9) + Water (1) = 10; Heaven (6) + Earth (2) + Thunder (3) = 11. The diagonals connecting opposite trigrams demonstrate the internal coherence of the system — they are not arbitrary but encode the polarity pairs of the Earlier Heaven arrangement.
